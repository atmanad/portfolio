# Design Specifications

## Hero Section (Hero3D.jsx)
- Floating animated cards showing: Name, Title, CTA
- Rotating 3D geometric shapes in background
- Smooth camera transitions
- Mouse tracking interaction

## Projects Section (ProjectsCard3D.jsx)
- 3D cards (uno-flip style or cube cards)
- Data source: `{name, description, tech, link}` from existing Projects.jsx
- On hover: expand and show details
- Click to navigate

## Skills Section (SkillsVisualization3D.jsx)
- 3D bars/pillars representing skill levels
- Labels: [skill name, proficiency %]
- Color gradient based on proficiency
- Data from existing SkillSet.jsx

## Color Scheme
- Primary: #0f3460
- Secondary: #00d4ff
- Accent: #16c784
